Dear my fellow musicians,
This is a website for you by you. it is simply a humble attempt by me to compile all the scattered oriental music sheets and lyrics in one place.
My main goal is to bring amators and professional music player together to share their knowledge and experience in one simple platform that is totally tailored to their needs.
I'll constantly update muzikji to rise to your expectations and to make your visit as welcoming as possible.
I want to thank everyone that helped in building this project directly or indirectly, starting with my lovely wife for her patience and support, my parents and my family. I also would like to thank all the great musician who created the score sheets. You are a blessing to us.

Mazen Aounallah
